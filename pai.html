BOSCH PAI - Smart Kitchen Projector


We work with Bosch to design the product - PAI to enrich existing living environments by creating new interaction and experience scenarios. This smart kitchen projector can be mounted underneath the kitchen wall cabinet or directly onto the wall, projecting a fully-fledged GUI on the kitchen worktop. A bright a nd high-contrast projection in combination with Orbbec integrated 3D sensor then “transforms” the kitchen worktop into a smart display.
