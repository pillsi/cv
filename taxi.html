Autonomous Taxi

Completed Self-Driving Taxi (concept)


Nowadays, many vehicle makers are in the development of self-driving car from stage L1 to L4 and ultimate goal to L5 which is completed non-human driving.
The concept of the antonomous taxi is based upon when L5 techbology has been widely used, and people may need a taxi robot as a role for covering all trasportation duties rathan than own a personal car. The autonomous taxi is just like self-driving Uber but different, passangers own entire privacy room and just need to control the system through smart phone or central display by setting route, connect free wifi and media, it is aiming to drive passagers safe, confortable and less spending, and provide a better interaction between machine and hunman.
